Numbers (Natural, Whole & Integers)

1. Natural Numbers

Definition: Natural numbers are counting numbers starting from 1.

Examples: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, ...

  • 1 apple
  • 5 books
  • 10 students

2. Whole Numbers

Definition: Whole numbers include 0 and all natural numbers.

Examples: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, ...

  • 0 pencils (none)
  • 7 days in a week
  • 15 chairs

3. Integers

Definition: Integers include negative numbers, zero, and positive numbers.

Examples: -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3

  • -5°C temperature
  • 0 floor (ground floor)
  • ₹200 profit, -₹50 loss
